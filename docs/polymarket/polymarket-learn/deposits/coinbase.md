Buying USDC
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**How to buy and deposit USDC to your Polymarket account using Coinbase.** Depositing directly to Polymarket from Coinbase is simple and easy. If you need help creating a Coinbase account, see their [guide on Coinbase.com](https://help.coinbase.com/en/coinbase/getting-started)

If something went wrong along the way, we recommend reaching to Coinbase support.
